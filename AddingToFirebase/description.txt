The Nianhaucang designed by ATL’s outdoor team is nominated in the shortlist of Low Budget Structures – 2022 [d]arc awards, while the Fangxia and Yunshu from indoor team is also in the shortlist of High Budget Places. The [d]arc awards are rooted in the arc and darc magazines with the reputation as being the most widely read and respected lighting design publications in the world. The shortlist has been chosen by an international jury of lighting designers.
